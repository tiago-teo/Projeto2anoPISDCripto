About Us

Our project brings together expertise in Data Protection, Internet Applications, and Distributed Systems to create a cutting-edge OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) platform.

This innovative solution automates the collection and centralization of critical information for penetration testing, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of cybersecurity operations for businesses. By leveraging data from diverse sources such as social networks, search engines, corporate websites, and leaked databases, our platform provides a comprehensive and accessible resource for identifying vulnerabilities and preventing cyber incidents.
