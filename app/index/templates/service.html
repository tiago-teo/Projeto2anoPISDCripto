How our service works

The project focuses on an online information search platform using OSINT techniques, in order to automate and centralise the search for relevant information for companies, increasing their efficiency and reducing the time spent obtaining useful data.
The Platform is capable of obtaining data from various sources, such as social networks, search engines, target company websites, database leaks, lists of known vulnerabilities and employee information.

User Friendly

The system is designed to be compatible with various devices and browsers, addressing heterogeneity issues. Additionally, the client-server architecture with geo-replicated capabilities ensures that the service can grow according to demand, maintaining performance and availability even with an increase in the number of users. These advantages make the service an essential tool for companies looking to improve their cybersecurity posture, providing efficient, secure, and user-friendly management of critical information for the prevention and response to security incidents.

Centralised Information

The service automates the collection of relevant information from multiple sources through OSINT, centralizing all data on a single platform. This significantly reduces the time required to find useful information, allowing companies to focus their resources on mitigation and problem resolution actions instead of wasting time on manual data searches. Reduction of Human Errors: Automation minimizes the possibility of human errors in data collection, ensuring that all relevant information is consistently and accurately captured and analyzed.

Advanced Security and Incident Management

The system includes advanced functionalities for detecting data leaks, allowing companies to be immediately alerted to any security breaches. This enables a quick response to mitigate damage and implement preventive measures. Secure Information Storage: Using robust encryption techniques (SHA-256 for passwords and AES-256-CBC for sensitive data), the service ensures that all stored information is protected against unauthorized access, guaranteeing data integrity and confidentiality.
